Baked Margarita Spaghetti Squash

Indredients

  • 1 Large Spaghetti Squash
  • 2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
  • 1 Large Roma Tomato, Finely Chopped
  • 2 Tbsp. Fresh Basil, Finely Chopped
  • 1/4 Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 Tbsp. Each Salt & Pepper
  • 1 tsp. Garlic Powder

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

"Dont get mad, get oven."

2

Using large knife or cleaver, slice the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise down the middle.

"Knife to meet you!"

3

Use a spoon to remove the seeds and center strings.

"See you spoon!"

4

Drizzle two halves with olive oil and then sprinkle with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

"Sodium fine!"

5

Place the squash, open side down on a cookie sheet and bake for 30 minutes.

"Squash goals"

6

Remove from the oven, check to see if the squash is soft and easily comes up with a fork into spaghetti like texture. If it is too hard to remove, cook for another 10 minutes. Otherwise, remove from the oven, scrape and fluff the stringy squash with a fork (leave the squash in the skin).

7

Turn the oven up to broil. Add the tomatoes and frehs basil into the squash, stir and top with mozarella cheese.

"From my head tomatoes!"

8

Place in the broiler for 3-4 minutes, or until cheese is melted and slightly brown. Then allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Enjoy :)
