2) All Trails
The website, All Trails is a database for hikes across the country. I enjoy how they include everything from short walks to long hard hikes and that the hikes are ranked and can be searched by location. While the website mostly depends on hiker reviews, there are enough users that the information seems accurate. I also enjoy how each hike webpage has the weather for that location, photos, reviews, a rating, and many other features.
